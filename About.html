About Me

I grew up loving history and law. By 5 years old I had memorized the entire Constitution and all the Presidents in order by middle name and height. All through growing up my desire for all things history and law became such a burning in me that I had to follow it's calling. As a result I knew I had to become a lawyer. Plus because of my big ears I knew that I had to make something of my life to stop being made fun of. Plus I thought suing people sounded fun and a way to get back at people. So I breezed through Law School and started this firm. But I am here to fight for your rights at whatever the cost is. I know it's a difficult world out there. I know there a lot of jerks that want to take advantage of you. But I'm here to protect you from those jerks.




So why don't you just sit back and relax and let me serve you in any legal help that you might need. I have extensive wisdom and knowledge in many diverse areas that will allow you to sue whoever or whatever your heart desires! I have been the lead employment lawyer for global security company with 50,000+ employees and $2 billion+ revenue in the United States. I have also been delivering actionable and practical legal advice to C-level executives and other management, HR professionals and field operations on a range of day-to-day labor and employment matters including: recruiting and hiring practices; employee performance management; leaves of absence; workplace accommodations; wage and hour compliance; compensation plans; separation agreements; non-compete, non-solicit and other restrictive covenant agreements; internal investigations; and investigations by government agencies.
